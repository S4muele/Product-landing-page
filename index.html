About Us

Our olive oil tells the story of an hundred years tradition. Our Olive Oil is made from olive sourced locally and hand harvested by our expert growers.

We cold press our olives withing 24 hours from picking them and bottle it straight away to preserve their original properties.

Our exquisite Prime Extra Virgin Olive Oil is pure gold, rich in natural nutrients that promote health and a natural life style.
